What is Study Visa?

A Student Visa or Study Permit is a formal document that authorizes an overseas student to study abroad. It is for study overseas aspirants seeking to pursue education abroad. Every nation has its immigration policies. These are variable in terms of evidence of funds, required documents, etc. Also, there are a few factors that are universal for all Study Visas.

You can plan your study abroad in advance through an exhaustive understanding of the Study Visa process. It will enable you to fulfill the process requirements without any issues.

The Student Visa Intakes

The Study visa intakes are referred to as Semesters at Universities. These are the time periods in which new students are accepted by Universities. The intakes normally differ from one nation to another.

Here are the intakes of a few major nations:

Canada
  • Fall intake - September
  • Winter intake - January
  • Spring intake - May
Australia
  • Semester I: Belatedly February/early March to belatedly May/early June
  • Semester II: Belatedly July/early August to November
UK
  • Fall intake - September/October
  • Winter intake – January/February

What are the Student Visa requirements?

Study Visa requirement depend on the nation:

Canada
  • Enrollment in a designated University
  • Evidence of funds for
    • Tuition fees
    • Living costs for your family and yourself
    • Return journey for your family and yourself
  • A Certificate from the Police for the absence of a criminal record
  • Have sound health and appear for the medical exam if required
  • Prove that you will exit Canada after your Study Permit expires
Australia
  • Enrollment in an Australian University in a full-time course
  • Evidence of GTE - Genuine Temporary Entrant
  • Offer certificate proving good health
  • Offer certificate proving good character
  • Passport and other complementary documents
Germany
  • Passport and other documents for Visa application Evidence of monetary stability
  • Evidence of enrollment in a University in Germany
  • Evidence of earlier education
  • Health Insurance
  • HZB - Entrance Qualification Exam
  • Certificate OF proficiency in German or English language

What are the Required Documents for a Study Visa?

The required documents for a Student Visa are:

  • Legal passport
  • Evidence of being a genuine student
  • Recipients of payment to the University Educational documents
  • Evidence of Funds
  • Statement from Bank
  • Letter for approval of a loan
  • Letters for Scholarship
  • Support affidavit
  • Test results for proficiency in the language
  • EPF/GPF statement
  • Report for evaluation of the property
  • Certificate from CA
  • Certificate for the capability of the loan
  • Letters from the bank

Canada Study Visa Application Process

The required documents for a Student Visa are:

  • Step 1: Obtain the Letter of Acceptance from the Canadian University
  • Step 2: Confirm the requirements for the Student Visa on the IRCC website
  • Step 3: File the application online on the website of IRCC
  • Step 4: You can file the documents offline or online
  • Step 5: Submit the application letter
  • Step 6: Offer evidence of adequate monetary resources
  • Step 7: Submit evidence that confirms the nonexistence of a criminal record
  • Step 8: Offer medical exam reports and documents related to health insurance
  • Step 9: Arrange all the documents needed for the Canada Study Visa
  • Step 10: Offer evidence of proficiency in the English language
  • Step 11: Provide your biometrics at the VAC - Visa Application Centre
  • Step 12: Make a payment of the fee for application at the VAC
  • Step 13: Attend the interview for your Visa
  • Step 14: Wait for the processing of your Visa

What is the Cost of the Student Visa?

The Study Visa cost is variable based on the nation

Study in UK
  • The UK General Student Visa application fee is Pound sterling 348
  • The Short-term Student Visa application fee is Pound sterling 97 for 6 months
  • The Short-term Student Visa application fee is Pound sterling 186 for 11 months
Study in Canada
  • The cost to apply for Canada Study Visa is CAD 150.
Study in Australia
  • The fee for Australia Student Visa application is AUD 620.

What is the Processing time for a Student Visa?

The Study Visa cost is variable based on the nation

Australia

Germany
