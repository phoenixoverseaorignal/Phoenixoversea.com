Documents necessary for UK Immigration

1. You have to appear for a proficiency test for English language such as IELTS or TOEFL

2. You have to be from a nation that is not a part of the EEA or European Economic Area

3. You must possess all the necessary documents, certificates for work experience, and other credentials required for applying for a job to immigrate to the UK

4. You must possess the funds required for supporting yourself in the UK during the initial years of the Student or Work Visa

5. You must also offer health and character certificates for further substantiating your eligibility for the applicable UK Visa

Eligibility to Apply for a UK Visa

The Government of UK announced an immigration system based on points in 2020. The chief aspects of this immigration system are:

  • Candidates from the EU and Non-EU nations will be treated on par for immigration to the UK
  • The points system is applicable for overseas skilled workers, highly skilled workers, and students seeking to move to the UK
  • The salary upper limit has been cut to £26,000 from £30,000 per annum
  • Applicants have to demonstrate their aptitude in communicating in English at A level on par
  • Highly skillful experts have to be accredited by an authority in the UK but an employment offer is not required
  • Overseas students seeking to study in the UK are included in the points-based system. They have to offer the confirmation of a Letter of Admission from an educational institution. Competency in English and adequate financial resources must also be demonstrated.
  • The minimum score needed to qualify for a UK Visa is 70 points

How to secure 70 points for UK Immigration?

You will get 50 points if you have an offer of a job from an employer in the UK and can interact in English. Further, you can earn extra 20 points through any of the following qualifications for qualifying for the UK Visa:

  • 20 Extra points of your job offer has an annual salary of a minimum of £26,000
  • 10 Extra points will be offered for Ph.D. and 20 extra points for a STEM filed Ph.D.
  • 20 Extra points will be offered for a job offer in a skill shortage position

What is the processing time for UK Visa?

99% of the standard applications for a UK Visa are decided in 10 working days. The maximum time taken will be 15 working days.

The processing time for Priority UK Visa applications is within 3 to 5 working days. The processing time for Super Priority UK Visa applications is 24 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

You must have a job offer from an approved UK employer before you apply for a Skilled Worker visa. Approved employers are also known as sponsors, because they are sponsoring you to come to or stay in the UK.

EU, EEA and Swiss citizens and other non-visa nationals do not require a visa to enter the UK when visiting the UK for up to 6 months. All migrants looking to enter the UK for other reasons (such as work or study) will need to apply for entry clearance in advance.
