How to migrate to Australia from India?

Australia remains a top destination for skilled and talented professionals seeking immigration to an advanced nation. It does thus not at all astonishing that rate of Australia Immigration increases year after year. The Government of Australia has developed many Visa Subclasses to facilitate immigration to Australia from across the world including from India. Some of these categories include:

  • Australia PR Visa
  • Australia Study Visa
  • Australia Visitor Visa
  • Australia Family Class Sponsorship Visa

Australia is among the best nations to immigrate overseas owing to several factors. The nation has implemented a new system of points to make it easier for eligible overseas nationals to obtain the Skilled Worker Visa.

We have here outlined a few of the major aspects of Australian immigration from India. These are the driving factors for millions of candidates to apply for an Australia PR Visa every year.

Why should you immigrate to Australia?

  • Australia is an advanced nation that has excellent infrastructure and career prospects
  • According to the US News & World Report, Australia is the ranked 7th globally for quality lifestyle and ranked 8th for receiving citizenship
  • Australia is ranked 3rd in the world for a comfortable retirement
  • The nation has a progressive immigration system based on points
  • Australia has altered the points system to make it easier for skilled immigrants to receive more points under the immigration points assessment
  • The nation is steadily offering nominations to suitable candidates for Skilled Australia Visa after assessing through invitation

What are the Australia immigration eligibilityrequirements?

Australia is renowned for its planned immigration system and simple immigration rules. Sponsored/Nominated and Skilled Immigration Visas are among the highly common Subclass Visas for PR applications.

We have here briefly outlined the eligibility requirements for the most universal Skilled Subclass Visas for Australia. The applicants have to be:

  • Less than the age of Forty-Five years for Sponsored/Nominated/Skilled Visas
  • The occupation has to be included in the suitable Skilled Occupation List of Australia
  • Obtain the assessment of skills from an accredited assessing authority
  • Obtain at least 6 bands score in all for language competencies in IELTS
  • Receive a nomination from a State if filing an application for a Visa nominated by a State
  • Offer documents pertaining to work experience and education
  • Offer a certificate of Medical Fitness
  • Offer evidence of good character through Police Clearance Certificate

Australia Immigration Process under General Skilled Migration Program

Australia has adopted a system based on points for accepting skilled overseas immigrants. So you will have to secure good points for qualifying for immigration to Australia.

You will have to secure at least 65 points in order to qualify for filing an application for any of the points-based GSM Subclass Visas. This is based on your education, age, work experience, language proficiency, etc.

But a higher score can enhance your selection chances. Australia regularly offers Invitation to Apply â ITAs for PR Visa through selection draws based on scores of applicants.

Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

You can file an application for any of the Permanent or Provisional Skilled Visa streams under the GSM as a skilled worker. Further, Australia has designed diverse Subclass Visas based on immigration requirements like Student Visas, Visitor Visas, Parent Visas, Spouse Visas, etc.

The least qualification needed to file an application PR Visa is a Graduate Degree. Higher educational qualifications can offer you higher points as the immigration system of Australia is points-based. You can thus enhance your chances of receiving the ITA for the Australia PR Visa.

The majority of the Skilled Worker Subclass Visas like 491, 190, or 189 require at least 12 months of work experience. The Government of Australia allocates you immigration points corresponding to the assessment of your work experience. So higher work experience will definitely help you to succeed in your application.

The Department of Home Affairs specifies an IELTS band score of at least 6 in 4 English language competencies to qualify for a GSM Visa application. But if you score higher than this, you can earn more immigration points.

In order to file an application for a Skilled Subclass Visa through the GSM program you have to secure a minimum of 65 points from a total of 100 points allocated for major selection factors.
